About Mark Your Picture

Why this tool?

Photographers, creators, and designers need a quick, trustworthy way to protect their work. Most watermark services require uploading your images to a third-party server — we think that's wrong. Your creative work belongs to you, and it should never have to leave your device just to add a simple watermark.

🔒 No Upload Policy

Your images never leave your device. Everything happens entirely in your browser using the HTML5 Canvas API. There is no server, no cloud storage, and no intermediate transfer of your files. What you load stays on your machine — period.

⚡ 100% Client-Side

There is no server processing your images. No data is transmitted, no account required, no tracking of your files. The app works fully offline once installed as a PWA, making it fast, private, and available even without an internet connection.

✏️ What you can do

Add custom text or logo watermarks with full control over position (including free drag), opacity, rotation, font, and size. Process a single image or an entire batch at once — the batch mode packages all watermarked results into a ZIP file ready for download. The original image format (JPG, PNG, WebP) is preserved.

Open Source

Mark Your Picture is free and open-source. You can inspect the code, contribute improvements, or self-host it on your own infrastructure.
